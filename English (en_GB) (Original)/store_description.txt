Voicify - Control your device with voice actions!

This is not an application like Siri or S-Voice! Voicify doesn't interact or reply your questions! It will execute your commands!

Setup your voice actions and you are ready to control your device with voice commands!

Features:

* Custom voice actions and commands
* Choose between different actions from enable/disable wifi, data, sync, bluetooth etc to simply launch applications or a quick call/sms
* Say whatever you want to voicify, if there isn't any action assigned to your command, you can quickly add it through an easy dialog!
* Access voicify through an additional (optional) launcher icon
* Premium users will also have the power to enable a quick widget or a floating shortcut!
* Quick search your content through a specific customizable keywork!

Voicify doesn't require root access and it's compatible with all stock and custom ROMs!
